IxD Process

My Goal

My goal is to create a game-themed portfolio website that captures my identity as both a creative and a developer. I want the design and content to reflect not only my skills but also my personality and passion for games and design. This site will blend aesthetic appeal, readability, and a clean structure to create an immersive, engaging experience for users.

I want visitors, especially potential employers, collaborators, or clients in the game development, art, and web industries to immediately feel the creativity and care that went into the site. Everything from the layout to the navigation will feel deliberate and styled like a playable interface, but without sacrificing usability or accessibility.

Core Features

  • Game-Inspired Visuals: Pixelated accents, neon UI elements, and smooth hover effects will make the site feel dynamic and playful.
  • Clear Navigation & Structure: Sticky navbars and breadcrumb navigation will help users keep track of where they are at all times.
  • Professional Presentation: Despite the playful theme, the structure and information presented will remain professional, making the site both fun and suitable for portfolio reviews.

Target Audience

  • This website is aimed at professionals and companies in:
  • Game Design and Development
  • Digital Art & Illustration
  • Web Design & Front-End Development

Interface Elements

  • Sticky Navbar: Constant top bar with hover effects and clear labels. Easy access to all sections.
  • Subnavigation Bars: On pages like Game Projects or Blog, users will have access to subcategories (like different game titles or blog topics).
  • Breadcrumb Navigation: Shows where users are and allows them to go backward easily.
  • Responsive Design: All pages scale correctly for phones and tablets, ensuring content is always legible and navigable.
  • Interactive Buttons: Mimicking game UI, buttons respond to hover with sounds or animations to create engagement.

User Flow

  • Sticky Navbar: The Navbar is will allow Users to navigate the website by using the Navbar to go to majour pages on the website.

    • Reflection

    Working on this project has shown me how design and code come together to tell a story, not just through content but through interface and structure. I’ve learned how important it is to balance creativity with clarity. Building a game themed website doesn’t mean sacrificing usability; it means elevating the experience so that the site itself becomes part of the portfolio. The IxD process guided me in planning out this site with real users in mind and gave me the clarity to make my website truly reflect me. This website isn't just a collection of my work it's part of it.
